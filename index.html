Brand: Bloom & Brew

Industry: Food & Beverage

Bloom & Brew is a herbal coffee brand crafted for corporate professionals seeking calm, focus, and balance in their daily routine. It redefines coffee culture by blending natural wellness with a refined, modern identity.

Key Features:
1. Targeted Audience: Corporate professionals or people with busy lives who want to at least have the privilege of having a calm morning with their favourite flavour of coffee beans.
2. Logo: The split coffee bean with a leaf visually communicates the fusion of coffee and herbal calm—perfectly aligned with the brand idea.
3. Colour Palette: Earthy browns and soft greens evoke nature, relaxation, and wellness—contrasting traditional "high-energy" coffee branding.
4. Typography: It blends editorial sophistication with human-centered clarity, positioning Bloom & Brew between premium coffee culture and modern wellness.

Brand: Solistice

Industry: Beauty & Skincare

Solistice is a sustainable skincare brand built on plant-based, sun-powered ingredients designed for all genders. It reflects a balance of nature and science through a calm, refined, and conscious brand identity.

The client requested a premium, gender-neutral brand identity for a sustainable, plant-based skincare line. They asked for a "quiet luxury" and minimalist aesthetic, utilizing an earthy color palette to convey calmness, refined nature, and intentional daily rituals.

Key Features:
1. Logo: The star element hints at the sun/solstice concept, reinforcing the idea of sun-powered ingredients without being literal.
2. Colour Palette: The muted green and warm beige palette conveys sustainability, calmness, and skin-friendly purity.
3. Typography: The typography and overall softness avoid overly feminine or masculine cues, making it inclusive and modern.
